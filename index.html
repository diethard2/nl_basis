Welcome to NL BASIS

At this moment NL Basis is a QGIS plugin still under development. It is a plugin that can be used to obtain a spatialite database on your computer with the basis geodata of the Netherlands that is also used by the Dutch public services.

I have been able to process one of these basis geodata, the BAG holding all the building information including adresses. Below you find how it looks after loading the result in QGIS (with PDOK-achtergrond BRT as a background layer provided in WMS and loaded using the QGIS PDOK plugin).

With a satellite background and making the layers pand and ligplaats transparent it becomes obvious that the boundaries provided are spot on!
